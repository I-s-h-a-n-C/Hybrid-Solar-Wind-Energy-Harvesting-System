An (as much as possible) compact modular hybrid renewable energy system that combines a wind turbine and a solar panel with integrated lithium battery storage. The system is designed for microgeneration, capturing energy from both wind and sunlight to provide stable, buffered power for low-voltage electronics and small-scale applications.

For a full guide on the project visit: https://www.instructables.com/Portable-Hybrid-Rapid-Deploy-Renewable-Generator
